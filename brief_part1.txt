1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
Build a complete, production-ready mobile application called HomeChef ÔÇö a homemade food marketplace that connects home-based cooks, bakers, and dessert makers with local customers looking for authentic, handcrafted meals.
This is NOT a restaurant or grocery delivery app. It is a personal, community-driven platform where home cooks share what they make, and customers discover real food from real people nearby.
The UI design is fully provided via the attached Stitch design file. Your job is to implement it completely ÔÇö pixel-perfect ÔÇö with all business logic, navigation flows, state management, real-time updates, and API integrations.
Core Concept
A home baker who makes donuts or tiramisu has no storefront ÔÇö they currently rely on social media and word of mouth. HomeChef gives them a professional platform to:
Post what they are making today with photos, price, and available quantity
Receive preparation requests for dishes they can cook on demand
Showcase their full specialty menu for advance pre-orders
Manage orders, earnings, and customer relationships in one place
Customers get to:
Discover homemade food nearby updated daily
Order before it sells out with real-time availability
Request custom preparation for a future date
Pre-order from a chef's specialty menu for any occasion

4. THE THREE ORDER MODES (CORE BUSINESS LOGIC)
This is the heart of the application. Three distinct ordering modes must coexist seamlessly within the same platform.
Mode 1 ÔÇö Instant Order (Daily Post)
The chef posts what they have prepared today. Customers order from what is currently available.
Chef publishes a daily post with photos, price, available quantity, and order deadline
Post appears live in all followers' feeds and nearby discovery feed
Real-time remaining quantity counter counts down as orders come in
Countdown timer shows time remaining until order deadline
When quantity reaches zero or deadline passes, post automatically shows Sold Out or Closed
Payment: Pay Now via card or Pay on Delivery
Mode 2 ÔÇö Preparation Request
The chef lists what they are capable of preparing. The customer requests a specific dish for a future date and the chef approves or rejects.
Chef maintains a Preparation Menu ÔÇö a list of dishes they can cook on request
Each item in the prep menu has: name, description, photos, base price, minimum order quantity, minimum notice time in hours
Customer browses the prep menu on the chef's profile and selects a dish
Customer fills a request form: desired date and time, quantity, special instructions, and budget if negotiable
Chef receives push notification with full request details
Chef can: Accept with confirmed price and time, Reject with an optional reason, or Counter-offer with a different price or date
Customer receives push notification with the chef's response
If accepted: order is created and payment is processed or scheduled
Full conversation thread between customer and chef on the request
Mode 3 ÔÇö Specialty Pre-Order
The chef's profile displays everything they are known for. Customers browse this catalog and place advance orders for any item regardless of whether it was posted today.
Chef maintains a Specialties Catalog ÔÇö their complete portfolio of offerings
Each specialty has: photos, description, price range, typical preparation time, availability (always available / seasonal / on request)
Customer taps any specialty item and selects a future date from a calendar picker
System checks chef's availability calendar and blocks unavailable dates
Order is submitted as a pre-order and chef confirms or suggests an alternative date
Ideal for occasions: birthdays, gatherings, events ÔÇö where planning ahead is necessary
