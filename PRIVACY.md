# HomeChef Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026

## Overview
HomeChef ("we", "us", "our") operates the HomeChef mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Full name, email address, phone number
- Profile photo (optional)
- Delivery address(es)

### Order Information
- Order history, payment records
- Delivery preferences
- Reviews and ratings you provide

### Location Data
- With your permission, we collect location data to show you nearby chefs and enable delivery tracking
- You can disable location access in your device settings

### Usage Data
- App interaction data, crash reports
- Device type, operating system version

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain our service
- To process orders and payments
- To show you nearby chefs and relevant specials
- To notify you about order status updates
- To improve our app experience

## Data Sharing
- **Payment processors** (Stripe) — for card payment processing
- **Delivery partners** — your delivery address for order fulfillment
- We **never sell** your personal data to third parties

## Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) and secure storage (Supabase with Row Level Security) to protect your data.

## Your Rights
- Access, update, or delete your personal data
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Request a copy of your data
- Delete your account

## Contact
For privacy inquiries: privacy@homechef.app

## Changes
We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy in the app.
